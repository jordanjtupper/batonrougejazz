About Jordan

Musician, Educator, Bassist

Jordan J. Tupper is a Baton Rouge-based professional musician with nearly two decades of experience performing, teaching, and leading in music. A vocalist and bassist, Jordan currently performs with multiple Baton Rouge groups across jazz, classic rock, and contemporary cover music.

With Standley, Tupper, & Petersen, Jordan plays upright bass in a cool-jazz setting built around timeless standards, tasteful volume, and a polished sound for restaurants, weddings, private events, corporate gatherings, and listening rooms.

Recognized as a 2024 Grammy Music Educator Award Quarterfinalist, Jordan brings both musical experience and educational leadership to his work. His background as a former band director and music educator shapes the way he approaches each performance: prepared, professional, collaborative, and focused on serving the room.
